BERT:
pre-trained model that uses transformer architecture to process and understand natural language
BERT base (110M P), BERT large (340M P)
BERT is bidirectional context understanding, good for missing words by considering left and right context.(sentiment analysis, question-answering, named entity recognition)
GPT is Autoregressive. means predict next word based on the preceding words. (conversations, chatbots)
BERT uses encoder part of transformer architecture because it is not for sequence-sequence tasks

def faq_bot(question):
    context = long_text
    input_ids = tokenizer.encode(question, context)
    tokens = tokenizer.convert_ids_to_tokens(input_ids)
    sep_idx = input_ids.index(tokenizer.sep_token_id)
    num_seg_a = sep_idx + 1
    num_seg_b = len(input_ids) - num_seg_a
    segment_ids = [0]*num_seg_a + [1]*num_seg_b
